WREXHAM fans were treated to another taste of Hollywood before their promotion sealing win over Boreham Wood.

That’s after Ant-Man star Paul Rudd turned up for a pre-match beer at the local pub.

Fans of Welsh side Wrexham have seen their fortunes transformed under American owners Rob McElhenney and Ryan Reynolds.

The A-list actors have taken the club to the National League title and promotion to League Two following their 3-1 win over Boreham Wood, as they saw off rivals Notts County in a thrilling title race.

Two goals from star striker Paul Mullin got Wrexham over the line having gone a goal down early on.

And they were cheered on their way by Friends star Rudd.
