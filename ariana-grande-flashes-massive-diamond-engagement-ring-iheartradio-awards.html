ARIANA Grande flashed her MASSIVE diamond engagement ring at the iHeartRadio Music Awards.

The 27-year-old showed off the ring after her secret wedding to Dalton Gomez.

Ariana joined The Weeknd on stage to kick off the iHeartRadio Music Awards on Thursday, as the paired performed his hit Save Your Tears.

While many viewers were impressed by the singer's impressive vocals, others couldn't take their attention off of Ariana's massive engagement ring.

Fans took to Twitter to gush over the singer and the gorgeous ring, as one person wrote said Ariana was "glowing."

Another added: "ARIANA HAS HER WEDDING RING ON I CANT."

A third chimed in: "ARIANA IS GLOWING WITH THAT WEDDING RING & OUTFIT."

Alongside a gif of an excited man, a following person added: "Me seeing ariana doing well and looking but she was wearing her wedding ring."

Others admitted they were on the lookout for the ring during the performance, as one viewer tweeted: "Not me looking for Ariana’s wedding ring."

An additional fan wrote: "Ariana’s wedding ring was shining on that stage like it was giving it’s own performance."

TMZ reported Ariana married Dalton in a secret ceremony earlier this month.

The pop star threw the bash at her $6.75M Montecito, California mansion and steered from tradition as there was “no real ceremony” and “only a few” guests. 

According to TMZ, the couple wanted “informal” nuptials, but it's unclear whether this was an impulsive decision. 

Ariana and Dalton were first linked in February 2020 after the Grammy winner split from Mikey Foster.

The singer's new husband has great taste, as Dalton helped design her "tasteful" wedding band.

As Dalton did for Ariana's engagement ring, the groom-to-be enlisted jeweler Solow & Co to design the wedding band, E! News reported.

The jeweler told the outlet that they made Ariana a "handmade diamond and platinum" band.

A spokesperson shared: "They worked with Dalton on this. It's a beautiful, tasteful platinum and diamond pave band. Dalton was very happy with it."
