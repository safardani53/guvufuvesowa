KANYE West has been enjoying an extravagant, extended visit to Dubai and the Middle East.

And the rapper apparently has no plans to come home any time soon, amid rumors of trouble in his marriage to Bianca Censori.

An insider told The U.S. Sun Kanye doesn't plan on coming home to reunite with his four children for Christmas.

Recent photos showed the 46-year-old partying with wife Bianca in Dubai, as they reunited after taking a "break" amid a reported rough patch in their marriage.

"Ye has loved being out of LA, he's never been a huge fan of the city and has no immediate plans to return, despite having a place here and his Yeezy HQ," the insider told The U.S. Sun.

"Bianca has spent time back in Australia and they're getting back on track and planning what to do for the holidays.

"There is talk of them returning to Italy in the next few weeks where he's renting a place and having Kim fly the kids there, but it's up in the air at the minute."

The U.S. Sun has reached out to Kanye's lawyer for comment, but in the meantime, the Vultures singer has been living it up.

DADDY-DAUGHTER DAY

Kanye was first spotted overseas earlier this month walking hand in hand with his daughter, North West, 10.

The father-daughter duo was spotted in Dubai, where Kanye was seen rocking his signature all-black look with a face covering and sock shoes.

North, the eldest child Kanye shares with his ex Kim Kardashian, 43, was seen in an oversized T-shirt and knee-length shorts.

The two were also spotted in and around Saudi Arabia, where they were seemingly greeted by fans.

While Kanye and Kim also share three other children - Chicago, five, Saint, seven, and Psalm, four - it seems as if North was the only one present on the trip.

LIVING THE LIFE

Kanye has made it a working vacation, focusing on new material with collaborator Ty Dolla $ign, and has already released the track Vultures.

Social media photographs show him working at a desert hideaway, and sources have revealed his new spot is the luxurious Banyan Tree.

The tent-like resort with state-of-the-art amenities is nestled in the canyons of Ashar Valley in AlUla, said to be "one of the world's most mysterious and exciting travel destinations."

It's a far cry from Kanye's more humble West Hollywood apartment near Melrose Avenue, which he had been renting before packing his bags and traveling the globe.

Kanye allegedly set up a music studio at the location, which offers one-bedroom villas for around $1,133 a night, along with larger accommodations.

FLYING SOLO

However, until recently, Kanye was a solo act.

He secretly tied the knot with Bianca, his Yeezy head architectural designer, just a month after his divorce was finalized.

But after being inseparable for months, they were said to suddenly be on a break, as the 28-year-old took her own solo trip to Australia.

Bianca's getaway came amid calls from her family to ditch her reportedly demanding husband.

An insider confessed to The U.S. Sun: "Bianca's family has never been a fan of Ye, and those close to her have questioned whether marrying him was the right decision.

"Kanye said they've been taking a breather since mid-October and his only focus right now is the music. He's keen to get this new record out with Ty Dollar $ign.

"He is a very difficult person to be around and work for, and Bianca has been one of the most patient people ever to deal with him. She's been all about Ye."

REUNITED

Kanye recently resurfaced in new photos with Chef Nobu Matsuhisa while visiting his swanky Dubai restaurant location.

Standing behind him was Bianca in a skintight bodysuit - and no bra.

But as eye-catching as Bianca may have been, Kanye was the one getting all the attention.

The father of four went completely barefoot in the snap, despite standing indoors at the high-end location.

The musician embraced Chef Nobu, who kicked out his feet to show off his eclectic new shoes - which were gifted from the controversial star.

A second shot showed Kanye shaking hands with the restauranteur in a rare happy moment caught on camera.

It turned out Kanye had taken off his Yeezy sock-shoes and given them to Nobu to try.

Nobu captioned his post: "The reason he was barefoot was because he gave me a pair of shoes of his design that he wore. #kanyewest @nobubythebeach @atlantistheroyal #nobuintown."

The barefoot rapper stood by as the Japanese chef slipped them on and showed them off.
