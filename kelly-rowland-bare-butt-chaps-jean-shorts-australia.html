Kelly has returned to the music scene with a bang, and reportedly has big things coming.

This summer, she reunited with Beyonce in London, with Beyonce on her Renaissance tour and Kelly in the country to headline the Might Hoopla music festival.

The Mighty Hoopla was Kelly's first show in the UK in 12 years.

Kelly reportedly wants to follow in Beyonce’s footsteps by dropping an album without warning.

The When Love Takes Over singer, who hasn’t released an album for a decade, has teased she has been working on music.

But rather than announce the project, she seemingly hopes to surprise fans with it, just like her Destiny's Child bandmate Bey did with her self-titled 2013 album.

Kelly said: "It’s spontaneity. Everybody knows everything now. There are no surprises and it’s kind of annoying to me."

Alongside wowing audiences on stage, Kelly is the busy mom of a young family.

The star has two children with her husband, Tim Weatherspoon: Titan Jewell, nine; and Noah Jon, two.

Titan was born on November 4, 2014, with Noah Jon joining the family on January 21, 2021.

In October 2020, during her pregnancy with Noah, Kelly told People: "Me and my husband were in the middle of COVID just like everybody else... and there's not much to do in the house.

"We were like, 'We'll just try for a baby, we'll see what happens because we don't know how long this is gonna last.' Literally, by the grace of God, God blessed us with a new life. We're just really excited."
