Ghosted A Romantic Comedy Movie Is Set To Released On Apple TV+

 Posted on May 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  487 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ghosted, Apple TV+’s latest romantic comedy will debut on the streaming app on April 21, 2023. The plot revolves around two individuals who meet under odd conditions. When one person falls in love with another, a startling discovery changes the situation drastically.

Chris Evans and Ana de Armas play the main parts, with several others portraying key secondary roles. It is directed by renowned filmmaker Dexter Fletcher.

Ghosted on Apple TV+: Teaser, storyline, what to anticipate, and more information revealed.

Apple TV+ has published the newest Ghosted video, which depicts the lives of the two main protagonists, Cole and Sadie. The video depicts the two figures having a pleasant exchange. The teaser also shows the pair enjoying some private moments while revealing what drew them together. Overall, it retains a profoundly impassioned tone that romance drama fans will surely appreciate.

One of the trailer’s standout features is the main couple’s rapport. Along with the preview, Apple TV+ posted the official movie synopsis on their YouTube account, which reads:

”Salt-of-the-earth Cole (Evans) goes head over heels for the mysterious Sadie (de Armas), only to learn she’s a covert operative. Cole and Sadie have whisked away on a foreign journey to save the world before they can settle on a second date.”

At this time, few other specifics about the tale are available. Still, based on the preview and description, viewers can expect an eccentric and enjoyable romance film that delves into a variety of fascinating themes such as love, adventure, and desire, among many others.

More information on the Ghosted characters

Ghosted’s main actors are Chris Evans and Ana de Armas. Cole Riggan is portrayed by Evans. Riggan is madly in love with an enigmatic lady called Sadie. However, his world is turned upside down when she ghosts him, shutting off all communication. He also learns that Sadie is a covert operative, which totally alters their relationship. Evans looks fantastic in the teaser, flawlessly capturing his character’s charisma and vibrancy. He successfully depicts the amorous enchantment. The thespian is expected to deliver an unforgettable performance.

Apart from Ghosted, Chris Evans is well-known for his roles in a variety of well-known and praised films and television programs, including Defending Jacob, The Red Sea Diving Retreat, and MCU films such as Avengers: Infinity War, Captain America: Civil War, and many more. Ana de Armas is similarly superb in the teaser, easily embodying her character’s mysterious atmosphere with extraordinary charisma. Armas’ portrayal defines the tone of the film, and her rapport with Evans takes the teaser to a whole new level.

Blonde, War Dogs, No Time to Die, and Una Rosa de Francia are among Ana de Armas’ other noteworthy film and television-playing accomplishments. Adrien Brody as Leveque, Marwan Kenzari as Marco, Mike Moh as Wagner, and many others round out the supporting ensemble. Remember to tune in to Apple TV+ on April 21, 2023, to view Ghosted.
