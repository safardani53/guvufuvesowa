CASHMERE is the best way to keep cosy in style as it's luxurious, lightweight and oh so snug.

Crafted from the softer-than-soft undercoat of a cashmere goat, the yarn is softer and lighter than sheep’s wool, and warmer. Need we say more?

Once upon a time, cashmere seemed like the height of luxury, wealth and expense. But now a range of high street retailers stock it, there’s no need to spend too much.

Whether you want a staple, immaculate jumper for your office wardrobe or a chic roll neck to accessorise those colder winter months, cashmere really can take you through any occasion depending on how you style it.

Marilyn Monroe loved a cashmere sweater - it's accessorised glamorous style icons through the years. But it doesn’t have to have an A list price tag - even Kate Middleton wears cashmere from high-street label Jigsaw.

Everyone deserves to afford cashmere, so we’ve done the hard work for you and rounded up a list of our fave bargains on the high street now. You’re welcome.

1. Uniqlo

For affordable, high quality basics, get to Uniqlo - the retailer's range of cashmere is best selling.

These men and women's sweaters are a reliable Uniqlo go-to, crafted from pure, luxurious cashmere. But there's a variety of other designs too, from V-necks to cardigans.

There's not only a range of cuts and fits, you can choose between an array of vibrant colourways. We love affordable luxe fashion.

2. Marks and Spencer

Marks and Spencer has a beautiful collection of pure cashmere hoodies, cardigans and jumpers.

Every piece is indulgently soft and cosy.

Crafted from machine washable cashmere - they’re everyday essentials for those colder months.

The cardigans are elegant, the V-neck sweaters are sophisticated and the hoodies are chic but still super snug.

Each style comes in a range of colourways, do you really need persuading?
