THE 1975 frontman Matty Healy is a versatile English musician who previously dated FKA Twigs for two years - but he's been spotted with a new American beau.

The Sun has exclusively revealed that Matty Healy has been dating Taylor Swift, and the pair are ready to make their relationship official soon.

Who is Matt Healy?

Like Taylor Swift, Matt Healy is also known for his career in the music industry.

He is an English singer, actor, and composer, who was born on April 8, 1989, in London.

Healy is arguably best known for his role as the singer and guitarist of the British award-winning band, The 1975.

The band won Best British Group at the Brits in 2017 and 2019, as well as British Album of the Year in 2019. In 2022, the group released their latest album, Being Funny In A Foreign Language.

The band formed when he and his bandmates - Adam Hann, Ross MacDonald, and George Daniel - attended Wilmslow High School in Cheshire.

Healy credits the likes of Michael Jackson, Talking Heads, and My Bloody Valentine as some of the band's musical influences.

When did Taylor Swift and Matty Healy get together?

It was revealed by a source close to Taylor that she and Matty are now seeing each other. The source told The Sun that "she and Matty are madly in love. It’s super-early days, but it feels right. They first dated, very briefly, almost ten years ago but timings just didn’t work out."

While this comes just weeks after Taylor and Joe Alwyn announced their break up, the source says that she and Joe Alwyn split in February, so there has not been any overlap between the breakup and her new romance with Healy.

Matty was spotted beneath a private tent at Taylor Swift's Nashville concert on May, 5, 2023, standing alongside American artists Julien Baker and Gracie Abrams.

Who are Matt Healy's parents?

While Healy achieved fame in his own right, his parents are familiar faces on our TV screens.

His mum Denise Welch, who was a panelist on Loose Women, is known for her acting roles in Coronation Street, Waterloo Road, and Soldier Soldier.

She previously opened up about the band's ballad, She Lays Down, which she says was written about her battle with postnatal depression and drug use.

Healy's dad Tim is also a TV legend, having starred in Auf Wiedersehen, Pet, and Benidorm.
