A BARBERSHOP customer is fighting for his life after being shot while having his hair cut today.

The man, in his 20s, was blasted at close range with what is thought to be a shotgun.

He was in the Katz Kutz barbers just after 5pm when men burst in and assaulted him.

The group left and came back with a gun before shooting the victim in the stomach in the Walsall barbershop.

Photos show blood and hair cuttings still on the floor as cops launch a probe into what happened earlier today.

Witnesses described hearing a shot fired, and a pal of the victim told the Express and Star he was "in theatre" this evening.

They described him as a "good kid", adding: "I just heard a big bang , it sounded like someone’s boiler had exploded or something. I thought it was someone’s house. It was very, very loud."

A West Midlands Police spokesman said: "The men ran off on foot and a police investigation is underway to identify and catch the offenders.

"Police have cordoned off the area to preserve any evidence - and anyone who saw what happened, or has any information on the shooting, is urged to contact West Midlands Police."
