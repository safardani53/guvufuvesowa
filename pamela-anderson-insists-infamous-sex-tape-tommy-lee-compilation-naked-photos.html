PAMELA Anderson has denied accusations that she made a "sex tape" with her ex-husband Tommy Lee.
The infamous video was leaked in 1995 but the former Baywatch actress has now insisted that the video "was not a sex tape" but simply "a compilation of vacations that we were naked on."

Speaking on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, Pammy's comment left bravo star Andy in tears of laughter.

He added: "I love that!"

Pamela and Tommy married in 1995 and made headlines soon after when the tape appeared online.

They split in 1998 after three years of marriage.

Pamela found fame on 90s TV show Baywatch, and she also took the time during her appearance on the show to slam Dwayne Johnson's recent big-screen reboot - even though her iconic character, C.J. Parker, made a cameo in the flick.

She said: “I didn’t like it.

“Let’s just keep the bad TV as bad TV. That’s what’s charming about Baywatch, you know?

"Trying to make these movies out of television, is just messing with it.”

The 52-year-old actress went on the 1990's television series made a big splash without spending big bucks.

She said: “$65 million would make a good movie. We made our show for like $500,000. You have the same explosions, the same sequences of water, that’s the fun part. Being creative.”

Pamela even said in an interview with The New York Times last year she still wears her famous red swimsuit from time to time.

For special dates she shared: “I jump in the shower with a bathing suit and then jump on them wherever they are in the house, soaking wet.”
