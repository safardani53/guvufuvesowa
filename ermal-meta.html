Ermal Meta is a singer, songwriter, and composer from Albania. He rose to prominence as the lead singer of the bands Ameba 4 and La Fame di Camilla. Ermal then went solo and has four solo studio albums to his credit. He got the MTV Europe Music Award for Best Italian Act in 2017. He won the Sanremo Music Festival the following year and went on to represent Italy in the 2018 Eurovision Song Contest.
