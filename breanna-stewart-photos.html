Breanna Stewart is a WNBA player who plays for the Seattle Storm. She has played for the Storm since 2016. Breanna was a college basketball player for UConn, where she was a 4-time NCAA champion. During her first year in the WNBA, she was named the WNBA Rookie of the Year. She helped lead the Storm to two WNBA championships in 2018 and 2020. She was the WNBA Finals MVP both years. Breanna has also been a coveted member of Team USA. She represented the United States at the 2016 Rio Olympics. Breanna was part of the gold-medal winning team. She is set to be a part of Team USA once again at the 2021 Tokyo Olympics. Breanna proposed to her longtime girlfriend, Phoenix Mercury player Marta Xargay Casademont, on May 2, 2021.
