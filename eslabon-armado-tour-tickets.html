How can I get Eslabon Armado tour tickets?

Who are the Eslabon Armado members?

Eslabon Armado are made up of two Mexican-American brothers.

The singer and frontman is 19 year old Pedro Tovar.

Pedro also writes the music - which he says he was inspired to do because of their dad.

A lot of the songs are románticas, or love songs, due to Pedro's love of romantic movies.

He describes their sound as traditional sierreña music.

Pedro is joined by his bass-playing younger brother Brian Tovar, who is 18 years old.

The band has had various other members since they began, including Gabriel Hidalgo who has since exited the group.

However, the two brothers have been constant throughout.

Now the band also has two other guitarists - Ulises Gonzalez and Damian Fidel Pacheco.
