TORI Roloff has shared an adorable video of her one-year-old son, Josiah, clapping his hands.

The Little People, Big World family attended a special event in Austin, Texas.

Tori and her family attended the Little People of America conference in Texas this week.

According to the website, LPA is a nonprofit organization that "provides support and information to people of short stature and their families."

Before the conference, it seems that the organization threw a dance party for all of its members.

Tori posted a video to her Instagram story on Friday night.

Josiah sat on the floor and clapped his hands as Mr. Brightside by The Killers played on the speakers.

Other people walked and stood behind him on the dance floor.

Josiah clapped really slow, almost to the beat of the music.

He looked around as Tori filmed him.

She captioned the post: "Si loves LPA."

Tori's third child is just a little over one year old and is already sitting up on his own and clapping.

CONFERENCE TIME

The LPA conference is being held from June 30 to July 7 in Texas.

Aside from the conference, LPA is hosting an Austinpalooza!

It will feature food trucks, street art, live music, and much more.

More events include cornhole, dances and socials, meet and greets, bingo, meetings and workshops, a fashion and talent show, and pool time.

This year's conference is its 66th annual convention.

JETSETTER

At the Roloff's traveled to Austin, Tori shared a video of her oldest son, Jackson, walking down a plane runway.

The six-year-old had surgery in 2021 to help correct the bowing in his legs.

Tori and husband Zach Roloff shared some future memories as the entire family boarded a plane for a summer vacation.

Besides Jackson and Josiah, the reality stars are also parents to Lilah, three.

Tori added several photos and videos of the start of their trip to her Instagram stories.

Among them were shots of the family sitting in their seats with wide smiles as they jetted away.

In a video, Tori followed Jackson and Lilah as they headed down the long boarding tube on the plane.

Jackson wore a backpack and had a blanket wrapped around his shoulders.

Lilah was right next to him, carrying two Frozen dolls with her.

As she walked, Lilah assured her mom that she would not get sick during he flight.
