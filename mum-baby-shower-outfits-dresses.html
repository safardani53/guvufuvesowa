Stylish mum-to-be shares baby shower outfit options but people are mortified by the same unusual dre

 Posted on June 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  369 words  |  Martina Birk

A TRENDY mum-to-be was savaged online after sharing a few of her outfit options for baby shower.

Ahead of the celebrations, TikTok user Megan Ellaby (@meganellaby) decided to turn to her 55k fans on the app to help her decide which dress to wear.

In the now-viral video, Megan, who is pregnant with a baby boy, showcased a total of four options, which were tried on one by one.

The first number was an off-shoulder puff sleeve mini dress in dark green, which the stylish mum-to-be had paired with trendy block heels.

The following item was perfect for Spring - an adorable mini dress in stunning lime green with wide plead detailing and balloon sleeves.

This doll-like garment was completed with a cute bow on the back, as Megan gave her viewers a twirl.

Third up was a baby blue tulle dress, which almost reminded of a cloud and was perfect for the theme of the party.

Just like the previous dresses, this too had plenty of space for the big bump that was hiding underneath the fabric.

But whilst Megan said she wished to ''keep them all'', social media users were not so sure - and there was one dress in particular that left them all mortified.
