DYLAN Dreyer has shocked fans by wearing a stunning plunging red dress to a lavish charity event.

The Today show meteorologist wowed in the revealing gown as she attended the Go Red For Women Red Dress Collection concert in New York City.

Dylan, 43, posed on the red carpet in the floor-length dress, which featured a pleated bodice and low-cut neckline.

She ditched her typical short hair for long straight extensions which were slicked back off of her face.

The mother-of-three completed her glamorous look with sky-high metallic heels.

Dylan posted several photos of her outfit on Instagram on Wednesday.

Dylan prepared for the glam event with a pampering session earlier in the day.

The weather reporter posted a selfie with a white sheet mask on her face.

"Big event tonight…gotta look my best!!" she explained.

Dylan's big night out comes after she moved away from the Today show to a different program by taking part in a golf tournament.

She played in the Hilton Grand Vacations Tournament of Champions, which aired on the Golf Channel on NBC's Sports Group.

Dylan proudly shared a video of herself hitting the ball away from the very edge of a stream.

She captioned it: "How’d YOU play today? I’ll spare you all my other shots, but this one was good!!"

While the weather reporter was happy with her shot, fans were more concerned about alligators potentially lurking in the water.

One commented: "Watch out for gators, we have a saying here in Florida, if there is water most likely there is a gator in it."

Another warned, "Yikes! Watch for alligators!" and a third added: "It looks like that ball was very close to a gator."

Her husband Brian acted as her caddy in the event.

Dylan told Hello! that her husband is her “biggest fan” and said it’s “really fun for him” to be the caddy.

She said: “If I make a good shot, he’s the first to jump up and down for me.

“He’s the perfect caddy because he’s in my head and he gets my mind right.”
