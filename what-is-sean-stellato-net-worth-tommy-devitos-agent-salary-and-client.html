Sean Stellato is among the most popular sports agents, earning a massive net worth. 

He recently made headlines with his outfit in a match between the New York Giants and Green Bay Packers.  

He was attired precisely as one would expect from an agent representing an individual with the moniker “The Passing Paisano.”

Sean P. Stellato, born April 12, 1978, is a certified NFLPA Agent / CFLPA Agent, motivational speaker, and author. 

The Salem, Massachusetts native was a player in the Salem High School, playing football and basketball.  

However, after a short-lived professional career in football, Stellato started working at a mid-sized sports agency.

He now owns Stellato Sports, a versatile agency specializing in sports representation and marketing.

Apart from managing a diverse portfolio of NFL players, Stellato Sports organizes skills camps and provides coaching for training and conditioning.

Additionally, the agency manages player events and promotions.
