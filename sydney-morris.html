Career

Sydney began gymnastics training when she was very young. She began competing in local gymnastics competitions after learning the fundamentals. Sydney quickly became a member of ‘The Coral Girls,’ a competitive gymnastics team based in Annapolis, Maryland. Because the sport necessitates lengthy training sessions, all members of this team are homeschooled.

The team is currently coached by professional gymnast Mary Thompson, who is also a blogger with an Instagram account called ‘@travelinghandstands.’ Sydney first competed in the ‘Win-Win Gymnastics Meet’ in 2010. At the age of six, she competed at level four and won the bars event.

Sydney continued to compete in various championships under this meet until 2014, eventually reaching level 7. She later transferred to ‘First State Gymnastics’ in Delaware. Then, with an all-around average of 44.200, she quickly qualified for the International Elite. She is currently undergoing level 10 training. Sydney’s talent lies in uneven bars, where she has excelled to date. She and two of her ‘Coral Girls’ coworkers, Peanut Ours and Hailey Merchant, are the only three members of the squad with two major releases on bars.
