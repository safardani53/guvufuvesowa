IF you haven't seen the hype about The White Company's festive winter candle, then you must've been living under a rock.

But if you don't want to fork out the whopping £95 price tag on the luxury candle, then you're in luck...because Lidl is selling a dupe that's an impressive £70 cheaper.

Delighted shopper Danielle Bromley took to the money-saving Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK after she spotted the almost identical replica in her high-street store.

Alongside an image of the candle dupe, she penned: "Lidl Large festive winter candle £25.

"Very similar to the White Company and John Lewis ones that are £95 and £63... apparently Aldi have them too."

She also shared a snap of the White Company 'Winter Indulgence Botanical Candle,' which retails at £95, and is currently out of stock - and it's fair to say the two bear a striking resemblance.
