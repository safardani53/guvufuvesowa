Denise Van Outen's ex fiance breaks silence on split saying they haven't spoken since break-up and G

 Posted on May 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  449 words  |  Aldo Pusey

DENISE Van Outen's ex-fiancé Eddie Boxshall has revealed the pair haven't spoken since their shock break-up in March.

The DJ previously apologised and voiced his regret at sexting other women behind Denise's back during their relationship.

He threw himself into the gym and his work after their bitter fallout and insists he wishes Denise well.

Speaking exclusively to the Sun at the premiere of The Last Heist, he said: "You know any break-up's hard, for whatever reason, and it makes it worse, you know, certain things get printed in the press. And there's two sides to every story."

Denise has found love again with Jimmy Barba, and the pair often share their date nights on social media.

Addressing her new romance, Eddie said: "There is no animosity. Life goes on, The world carries on going round. She's happy. I'm really happy she's moved on. And I wish all the love and luck in the world.

"You know, I'm still yet to start dating. I'm looking at career wise first. Once I'm ready to move on, I'll move on. Thankfully, you see you want to try."

Eddie and Denise were a popular pairing on Celebrity Gogglebox but quit the show after their split.

Despite not being keen to do the show at first, Eddie enjoyed his two years reacting to popular telly shows and was sad the experience came to an end.

He said: "It was something for about two years, being asked to do, you know, Denise said about doing it and because it was wasn't my forte, it wasn't my world I sort of, you know, sidestepped it. And to the point, I was just like, you know what, I'm gonna give it a go. And when I did, I felt really enjoyed it.

"The team, everyone that works on the channe 4, everyone on the production side of the show itself are fantastic. 

"And again, you know, a little bit nerve wracking sitting in front of the camera. Myself and Denise when we were together, we were big fans before it, for the for the regular one to be part of the celebrity one was was huge for us.
