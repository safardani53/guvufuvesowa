CHIEF Keef’s baby mama Slim Danger is an adult actress who hooked up with the rapper after setting her sights on him at one of his concerts. 

The porn star recently made controversial allegations about NFL wide receiver Odell Beckham Jr's sexual preferences.

Here is more on Slim and why she's in the headlines again.

Who is Slim Danger?

Born Aereon Clark, Slim Danger is an adult actress and pornographic model. 

The 31-year-old rose to popularity onOnlyFans and claims to have gotten her name from R&B singer Ray J’s 2009 reality show, For The Love of Ray J, which featured a contestant named Danger. 

The show popularized a chant by former MTV star Tom Green, who said “Danger, she smashed the homie!” after discovering that Danger had slept with one of Ray J’s friends.

Slim says she decided at that moment to use the moniker going forward. 

What podcast does she host?

Slim hosts a postcast called Thots Next Door with social media personalities Celina Powell and Alzina. 

On the show, the women discuss their lives and trending pop culture topics, and in one episode Slim confessed to how she met her baby daddy and ended up pregnant.

How did Slim Danger and Chief Keef meet?

Slim claims she met Chief Keef after sending him a message and a video of herself on Instagram.

She allegedly told him she was attending one of his concerts in hopes that the two would hook up. 

It's unclear whether the two met for the first time that night or if they met at a later date.

Does Slim Danger have any children?

Slim is mom to a 12-year-old son from a previous relationship and four-year-old Zinc.

On her podcast, Slim claimed she “manifested” the pregnancy saying: “I didn’t get pregnant yet, so I was like, ‘I gotta do a little bit more manifestation. I got to get pregnant.”

She revealed she discovered she was pregnant once she went to California and let Chief Keef know. 

“I told him. I was like, ‘Yeah, I’m pregnant’ and he said, ‘Join the crew.'”

The two are reportedly not together but are instead working on co-parenting Zinc. 

What did she say about Odell Beckham Junior? 

Slim told listeners on Adam22's No Jumper podcast a story about Cleveland Browns star Odell Beckham Jr and his alleged preference for being defecated on while having sex.

While OBJ has yet to address the rumors, he quickly became a trending topic and sparked more than 72,000 mentions on Twitter. 

Slim also claimed in the video that OBJ allegedly asked her for a video of her pooping and requested that she “not shower for 24 hours or wear underwear” before getting on a plane to see him. 

"This is how I got flown out. He said take a picture of you s*******. So I said, alright, I can do that. I took a whole video, b****, what you want?”

"I sent it to him, and within the next two hours I was in Houston. I made it as sexy as possible," she said. 

Slim Danger claimed that she was ultimately unable to follow through with the act when she met Beckham.
