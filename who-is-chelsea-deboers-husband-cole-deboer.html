Who is Chelsea DeBoer's husband, Cole DeBoer?

TEEN MOM 2 star Chelsea Houska DeBoer found her happily ever after while she was at the gas pump filling up her car in 2014.

That's when she met her future husband Cole, with whom she now has a new kind of show in the works.

Who is Chelsea DeBoer's husband Cole DeBoer?

Chelsea met Cole at a gas station in South Dakota in 2014.

She admitted that she couldn't bring herself to say "hi" in real life, but that, luckily, a few days later, he found her on social media.

Cole has previously worked as a traffic controller, part of city planning.

He also owns a sock brand.

Most recently, it was announced that he would be co-starring beside his wife in an HGTV show focusing on the couple's home design and renovation business.

According to US Weekly, the show is tentatively called Farmhouse Fabulous.

It's slated to come to HGTV sometime in early 2023.

How long have Chelsea DeBoer and Cole DeBoer been together?

Chelsea and Cole have been in a relationship since 2014, but they tied the knot in 2016.

The duo got engaged in 2015 after Cole asked the most important person in Chelsea's life for permission; her daughter, Aubree.

“Cole asked Aubree if it was okay if he married her mommy,” Chelsea said on the MTV show.

“She immediately said yes, was extremely excited, and gave him a big hug.”

While Cole isn't Aubree's biological father, he has certainly stepped up to the plate as her stepfather.

A memorable moment from the Teen Mom 2 show was when Chelsea had Aubree's last name changed Lind-DeBoer, and Cole reacted by saying he'd adopt Aubree "right now" if it's what she wanted.

Do Chelsea DeBoer and Cole DeBoer have children together?

Chelsea and Cole DeBoer have three children together, in addition to Aubree.

Life for the family of six may not be on MTV anymore, but it will soon be live again on HGTV for viewers interested in what they've been up to.

As for whether or not they want more kids, the MTV alum said in a July 8, 2021, Instagram Question and Answer session that it was the most asked question that she and Cole always get.

Her answer: “Do we think we’re gonna have more kids? I would say we don’t think we’re gonna have more.”
