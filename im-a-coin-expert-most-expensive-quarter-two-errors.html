I'm a coin expert - Most expensive 2022 quarter sells for almost $700 - the two errors that you need

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  328 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

THIS coin expert details the two major errors that you need to look for to find the most expensive 2022 quarter, as one sold for almost $700 with the same mistakes.

According to a video from Eric Miller, a coin aficionado who goes by the username @thecoinchannel on TikTok, two specific flaws could be found in some 2022 quarters.

If you find one with similar mistakes, it could possibly earn you hundreds of dollars in return.

"The most expensive 2022 quarter error just sold yesterday," Miller explains.

He also included an image of a bidding war on the quarter that ended at $687.88.

Not many people know about the mistakes, per Miller.

"No one's gonna tell you about this, but I am, so make sure you save this video," Miller notes.

The first mistake

Initially, Miller just says to search through your pocket change, as there could be a 2022 quarter in there somewhere, maybe even a few.

"The reverse of the coin needs to be Wilma Mankiller," he says.

He then explains that there will be a dye crack at the top of the quarter on the heads-side where "liberty" is spelled out.

"The first one I've told you about, the dye crack in liberty."

The second mistake

However, Miller says that the most important flaw in the errored 2022 quarter is on the Wilma Mankiller tails side.

"But, when you turn it over, if you see a dye crack like this going across the reverse, then you just found the most expensive 2022 quarter error that is literally brand new," Miller says.

Miller says that anyone who views the video is now the first to know about the mistake that could make them a lot of extra cash.

Coin errors happen fairly often, and some older errors from the 20th century still for hundreds and even thousands of dollars, per Gainesville Coins.
