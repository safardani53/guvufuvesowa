Felipe Neto Rodrigues Vieira is a Brazilian Portuguese-born Brazilian manager, vlogger, actor, comedian, and writer. He is a popular YouTuber in Brazil with over 41 million subscribers, making his channel the 36th most subscribed channel on the site. He started his Youtuber career talking about celebrities, movies, daily critical activities or comics, being the first YouTube channel to reach 17 million subscribers in Brazil. He is also the brother of YouTuber Luccas Neto. Currently, his videos focus on integrated entertainment. Felipe is the founder of Paramaker, a network company within YouTube that owns channels like Parafernalha, IGN Brasil Network, and manages around 5,000 channels, with the aim of professionalizing the online video market in Brazil. He sold the company in 2015 to focus on his YouTube channel. Felipe was also involved in other businesses when he and his brother set up "Neto's" a food stand that sells "coxinhas", a popular Brazilian dish.
