Burger King adds new twist to popular side items but there's a catch

 Posted on May 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  361 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

BURGER King has made a big change to its menu, but you'll have to act fast to try it.

Diners will soon be able to order a brand new side with their Whoppers and Chicken Fries.

The fast food joint announced it will be debuting Fries n' Rings, a side option that comes with both french fries and onion rings.

The combo order will launch on June 19, but not everyone will be able to order it.

Only select Burger King locations in Miami and Fort Lauderdale, Florida, will get the new combo.

Customers will have to act fast as it will only be available through July 20.

The newest option can be ordered on its own or as a side or as part of a meal in sizes small, medium, and large.

Burger King currently has onion rings listed on its menu, but they can only be ordered by themselves as a side.

BIG CHANGES AT BK

Burger King is adding a ton of new menu items to spice up the summer.

First up are the Fiery Nuggets, offering a new spin on a BK classic.

These nuggets are coated in a spicy glaze that contains cayenne peppers, bird’s eye chili, and black pepper.

“Heat is definitely having a moment, so this felt like the perfect time to introduce the Fiery Nuggets with the spicy glaze we know our guests love,” said Chad Brauze, senior director of culinary innovation and sustainability, Burger King North America in a statement.

Customers who don't want something too spicy can opt for the $1 Frozen Fanta Kickin Mango.

The slushy combines mango and citrus flavors, with a subtle burst of chili spice.

“The Fiery Nuggets needed an equally tasty companion, so we’re excited to introduce this (combo) that simultaneously cools and kicks up the heat,” Dane Callis, senior brand manager, Fanta North America, said in a statement.

All participating locations will sell the two new items from June 19 through August 17.

Unfortunately, Burger King also added an automatic fee that customers can't avoid.
