'I cant get over these bags' - Brides rush to buy new hen do and wedding accessories in Quiz

THE wedding dress is the most important clothing purchase a bride will make - but it's not the only one.

From the white hen do outfit to veil accessories and embellished ivory handbags, there's always an excuse to fill your shopping basket.

So fashion fans have been rejoicing after high street store Quiz launched a stylish ivory satin dress with bow detailing.

The retailer has launched a hen do and wedding range with prices starting from just £4.99.

Posting on TikTok, Quiz said: "Calling all brides-to-be! Get ready to have the ultimate send off in style."

They added: "Add the finishing touches to your look with a pair of a statement heels or an embellished bridal bag.

"Take your hen party to the next level with our fun bride-to-be accessories such as headbands, hats and sunglasses – make sure it’s a night to remember."

And brides have already been racing to get their hands on some of the items.

One said: "I have the ‘I Do’ bag and the quality is uhhmazing."

A second wrote: "And just like that I’m ordering the wifey bag and necklace.
