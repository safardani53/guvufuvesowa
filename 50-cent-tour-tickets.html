50 Cent tour 2023: How can I get tickets?

 Posted on March 31, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  773 words  |  Aldo Pusey

50 CENT will be hitting the road this summer for a world tour with special guests Busta Rhymes and Jeremih.

The tour will kick off on July 21, 2023, in Salt Lake City, Utah, and run through November.

How can I get tickets for 50 Cent's tour?

50 Cent announced his upcoming tour on May 4, 2023, but fans will have to wait a few more days before they can purchase tickets.

Presale tickets are scheduled to go on sale on May 10 and then general public tickets will be released on May 12.

The tickets can be purchased on 50 Cent's website or on Ticketmaster.

Who is 50 Cent?

Curtis James Jackson III, who goes by the stage name 50 Cent, is a rapper, actor, television producer, and businessman, who was born on July 6, 1975, in South Jamacia, New York, NY.

He first rose to fame in the early 2000s after the release of his 2003 album Get Rich or Die Tryin'.

Since then, he has released five additional albums and countless songs.

50 Cent has also been seen in several film and television productions, including Power and For Life.

When 50 Cent is not in the studio or on set, he can often be found at home, spending time with his girlfriend, Jamira Haines, and his kids, Marquise and Sire.
