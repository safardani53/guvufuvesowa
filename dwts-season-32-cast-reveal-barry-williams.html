DANCING With the Stars has unveiled its new line-up for Season 32.

All of the contestants and pros were announced on Good Morning America on Wednesday morning.

The new season will air on Tuesday, September 26 at 8/7c.

The first contestant that was announced for Season 32 was Vanderpump Rules star, Ariana Madix.

She is going to be partnered with Pasha Pashkov.

Then, one the finale of The Bachelorette, Charity Lawson turned out to be the second contestant this season.

Charity will be dancing with Artem Chigvintsev.

Before the full cast announcement, the third competitor was announced on Tuesday.

They turned out to be Jamie Lynn Spears.

Britney Spears' younger sister is partnered with Alan Bersten.

WHO ELSE IS DANCING?

The other DWTS contestants were revealed on Wednesday morning.

I'm Yours singer Jason Mraz will be partnered with Daniella Karagach.

NFL star Adrian Peterson will be dancing with Britt Stewart.

Oscar-winner Mira Sorvino and Gleb Savchenko will be partnered together this season.

Actress Alyson Hannigan and Sasha Farber will be dancing together.

Marvel actress Xochitl Gomez is on this season with Val Chmerkovskiy.

Brady Bunch star Barry Williams is partnered with Peta Murgatroyd.

Veep actor Matt Walsh is with Koko Iwasaki.

Social media star Lele Pons will be dancing with Brandon Armstrong.

Real Housewives star Mauricio Umansky is dancing with Emma Slater.

Actor and model Tyson Beckford is also on this season and will be dancing with Jenna Johnson.

Reality star and YouTuber Harry Jowsey is with new pro, Rylee, who is Lindsay Arnold's younger sister.

STEP BALL-ROOM CHANGE

This is the first time in the history of the show that DWTS will air solely on Tuesdays.

The announcement was made on September 12 by Variety.

The outlet also reported that the premiere on September 26 will be two-and-a-half hours long.

For the rest of the season, DWTS will air at 8pm for two hours.

Since 2007, the competition show has aired on Mondays, but this year Monday Night Football will interfere with the first few episodes so ABC changed it.

DWTS used to air on Mondays and Tuesdays, with Tuesdays being the results show.

Then, it moved to just Mondays in 2013.
