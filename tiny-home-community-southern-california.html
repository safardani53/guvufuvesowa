A MANUFACTURED housing expert has shared a glimpse of a rare community full of tiny homes.

Just blocks away from the beach, Trailer Rancho is a dream space for anyone looking for the California lifestyle on a budget.

Homes in Encinitas range from 1.2million to 1.9million, but this expert has identified an incredible opportunity for prospective buyers on a budget.

Shane Joffe took his YouTube subscribers on a tour of the incredible community.

The video opens with a clip of rolling ocean waves alongside the coastal highway.

"I'm at Leucadia, which is one of the best surfing spots in southern California," Joffe noted.

"We have a community just blocks away with several new tiny homes available," he exclaimed.

According to the real estate expert, the area is one of the hottest spots in California.

Before heading to Trailer Rancho, Joffe stopped in the nearby town of Leucadia for a bite to eat.

The district was filled with wide streets, stores, and restaurants with outdoor seating, giving residents plenty of ways to enjoy Southern California.

Joffe decided to stop at a local pizza parlor to test out the dining options in the area.

"It's awesome. The ambiance is awesome. They have a great selection of beers. Look at all of this artwork. It's very cool," he added.

After finishing his meal, Joffe headed to the iconic tiny home community.

"All right now, I'm standing at Trailer Rancho," Joffe announced.

"Behind me is a very cool patio area with a canopy to protect you from the California sunshine so you can enjoy the ocean breeze. The ocean is literally right over there."

The real estate expert displayed the palm tree-lined streets, filled with various tiny homes, each offering their unique style and floor plan.

Joffe said that the tiny homes go for as much as $110,000, just a fraction of the typical home in Encinitas.
