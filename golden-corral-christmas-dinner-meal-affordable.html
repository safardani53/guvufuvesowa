Golden Corral is selling a Christmas meal for $15 per person - it feeds 8 and comes 'ready to eat'

 Posted on March 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  358 words  |  Aldo Pusey

CHRISTMAS dinner just got easier thanks to Golden Corral's affordable meal that feeds eight.

Preparing Christmas dinner can be more stressful than shopping for presents and wrapping all the gifts.

Couple that with the ever-rising cost of groceries and the holiday meal can be an impossible feat for some families.

Many people have chosen to order whole meals that are ready-to-eat to save time and money.

One affordable option comes from Golden Corral, where diners can get a meal to serve six to eight people for just $119.99.

Customers can choose to get it ready to eat or heat it at home on their own.

It is truly a feast and comes with a whole roasted turkey, mashed potatoes, gravy, stuffing, cranberry sauce, a dozen yeast rolls with honey butter, and a choice of sides and a whole pie.

Diners can choose brown gravy or poultry gravy.

The choices for sides are buttered corn, green beans, macaroni and cheese, steamed broccoli, steamed carrots, or sweet potato casserole.

For pies, the options are pumpkin or pecan.

Golden Corral also offers a ham dinner for $129 and a beef roast for $139.

ANOTHER AFFORDABLE OPTION

For those who enjoy cooking, Target is offering all the fixings for a meal for four for just $25.

The deal is meant to help shoppers “plan, prep, and enjoy a feast with loved ones” all without breaking the bank.

"Our guests turn to Target during special seasonal moments for that combination of value and joy that sets us apart," said Rick Gomez, chief food and beverage officer, at Target.

The meal comes with a 6-pound bone-in ham, potatoes, corn, bread, green beans, and more.

Customers can add additional sides for $5, like Good & Gather broccoli and cheese veggie tots and roasted frozen sweet potato chunks.

Desserts are also $5 and people can choose from the Favorite Day Holiday Peppermint Bark or Holiday Sea Salt & Milk Chocolate Caramels.

Whole Foods also has an affordable option that will feed eight people for $21 per person.

Cracker Barrel is also offering a Christmas feast that feeds 10.
