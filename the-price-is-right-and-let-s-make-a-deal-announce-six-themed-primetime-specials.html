The Price Is Right And Let's Make A Deal Announce Six Themed Primetime Specials

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  545 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The first two specials air on Tuesday, Oct. 27 with both shows paying tribute to essential workers with back-to-back episodes on CBS and CBS All Access.

Fall is here and while daylight gets shorter and the night gets longer, we've got six reasons to look forward to the evening!

Your favorite top-rated daytime game shows are headed into primetime as The Price Is Right, hosted by Drew Carey, and Let's Make A Deal, hosted by Wayne Brady, each announces three new themed specials to be broadcast in October, November, and December.

STREAM: Your Favorite Daytime Shows On CBS All Access

In addition to both shows revealing newly redesigned, socially distanced sets, Let's Make A Deal—in its primetime debut!—will feature a virtual wall with contestants from across the country and prizing that’s bigger than anything the show has seen in its 57-year run. Also, traders will have a shot at winning $100K hidden in golden envelopes throughout each special.

Here is the schedule for four of these exciting fall specials, with future dates for each show's third special to be announced at a later date:

Tuesday, Oct. 27
The Price Is Right At Night airs at 8/7c and Let's Make A Deal Primetime airs at 9/8c on CBS with back-to-back episodes saluting front line and essential workers by welcoming them to play on the iconic game shows.

The Price Is Right host Drew Carey thanks the front line workers for their dedicated service to communities when contestants have the opportunity to play favorite games, including “Cliff Hangers,” and win amazing prizes, such as luxury cars, a travel trailer, and cash. On Let's Make A Deal, host Wayne Brady will perform an opening number and the contestants will be comprised of essential workers. Traders will play “Smash for Cash” and “Car Pong,” and have a chance to win luxury cars, a recreational vehicle, and up to $100K.

Monday, Nov. 2
The Price Is Right At Night welcomes the cast of the hit CBS comedy The Neighborhood to “come on down” at 8/7c.

For the first time ever, stars play all six games as individual contestants when Cedric the Entertainer, Max Greenfield, Beth Behrs, Tichina Arnold, Sheaun McKinney, Marcel Spears, and Hank Greenspan test their knowledge of prices for The Boys and Girls Club of America. In addition, fans at home will have the chance to win fabulous prizes, including a showcase, by entering at www.priceisright.com.

Monday, Dec. 21
Let's Make A Deal Primetime airs at 8/7c and will be decked out for the holiday with Christmas decorations and festively themed games and deals.

On a third Let's Make A Deal Primetime episode, special guest Phil Keoghan, host of The Amazing Race and Tough As Nails, will present one lucky trader with an outdoor adventure-themed trip of a lifetime. The airdate for this episode will be announced at a later date. Likewise, details for the final The Price Is Right At Night special of 2020 will be announced at a later date.

The Price Is Right and Let's Make A Deal premiere their 49th and 12th daytime seasons, respectively, on Monday, Nov. 16.

Tune in for these exciting primetime specials and watch The Price Is Right and Let's Make A Deal weekdays on CBS and CBS All Access.
