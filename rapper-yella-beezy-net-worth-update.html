What is Yella Beezy?

Born in October 1991, Yella Beezy's real name is Markies Deandre Conway.

Before finding fame on the hip-hop scene, most of his early life was centered in Dallas, Texas.

In 2015, his career received traction after the success of his single, Trap in Designer, from his second mixtape - Broke Nights Rich Days.

Since 2015, the rap star has released five more mixtapes.

His 2018 mixtape Ain't No Goin' Bacc, reached No. 24 on the Billboard 200 chart.
