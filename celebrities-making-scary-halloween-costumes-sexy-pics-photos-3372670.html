Throughout the years, many celebrities, including Ariel Winter, Jennifer Lopez and more, have wowed us with their super impressive scary yet sexy Halloween costumes. From goblins to ghouls and every creature in between, there have been plenty of sexy takes on classically creepy creatures that’ll give you the perfect inspiration for your own scary, sexy look this year. Let’s take a look back at some of our favorites!

All eyes were on Winnie Harlow when she decided to dress up like a sexy demon with a hint of RuPaul Charles’ glamour for Halloween in 2018. The model’s costume consisted of a red leotard with thigh-high latex red boots and a voluminous blonde wig. She also wore a black coat that simply draped on her arms as she walked into Heidi Klum‘s 19th annual Halloween party at LAVO NYC in New York City on October 31, 2018. Like Winnie, Karlie Kloss put her own spin on this vampire costume in 2019 when she showed up to Heidi Klum’s Halloween party. Karlie rocked a pair of knee-high black boots with a sexy, strapless latex dress. The stunner let everyone know that she was out for blood by sporting the iconic Dracula cape. Ever the fashionista, Karlie accessorized with a long black wig that fit her perfectly. She turned the pavement into her own personal runway upon striding into the venue! Blac Chyna also got into the holiday spirit with her Halloween look on October 29, 2019. She went for an all black ensemble and added a few hints of makeup for her look, which paid homage to Mexico’s annual Day of the Dead festival. Chyna wore a skull headdress with a revealing black velvet jumpsuit for the Halloween bash. She added minimal makeup, save for some spooky black paint around one of her eyes.
