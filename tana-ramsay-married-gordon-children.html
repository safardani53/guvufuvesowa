Who is Gordon Ramsays wife Tana and when did they marry? The US Sun

 Posted on May 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  512 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

GORDON RAMSAY has enjoyed an incredible career as a celebrity chef, and has various TV projects to his name.

Throughout his fame and success he has had beloved wife Tana Ramsay, who he shares his many children with, by his side.

Who is Tana Ramsay?

Tana Ramsay was born in Croydon, London, on August 23, 1974.

She was born Cayetana Elizabeth Hutcheson and trained as a school teacher.

After meeting and marrying Gordon, Tana joined him in the world of showbiz.

Tana is the author of several cookbooks and has also made numerous TV appearances, including a stint on Dancing On Ice in 2010.

She also used to own a beauty salon in London called All About the Girl but as of 2023 it appears to be closed.

How many children do Tana and Gordon have?

As of November 2023, Tana and Gordon share six children.

Their sixth child, a little boy, was born in November 2023, with proud dad Gordon sharing the happy news on Instagram.

After uploading a picture of Tana in her hospital bed with the newborn Gordon wrote: "What an amazing birthday present please welcome Jesse James Ramsay, 7lbs 10oz whopper!!

"One more bundle of love to the Ramsay brigade!!

"3 boys, 3 girls…. Done."

Jesse is a little brother to Megan, who was born in 1998, twins Jack and Holly, born in 2000, and Strictly Come Dancing star Tilly who was born in 2001.

Jesse's closest sibling is his brother, Tana and Gordon's fifth child, Oscar James, who was born in April 2019.

Gordon had previously hinted he and his wife could add to their brood, revealing in January 2023 that Tana would love another child.

How long has Tana Ramsay been married to Gordon?

Tana and Gordon were married on December 21, 1996, which means they will celebrate their 27th wedding anniversary in 2023.

The couple live in Wandsworth Common in London but split their time between the UK and LA.

They also have three holiday homes in Cornwall, which total at a whopping £10million.

Tana and Gordon are good friends with the Beckhams, who they often go on holiday with, and Tana and Victoria are particularly close.

What does Tana Ramsay do as a job?

Tana's is an author and TV star.

From 2015 until 2019, she starred in her daughter Tilly's show Matilda And The Ramsay Bunch on CBBC.

Tana has also been a presenter on UKTV's food show Market Kitchen, and appeared on the US version of MasterChef, co-hosted by her husband, Gordon.

Tana is very active on social media, and can make around £1,000 a sponsored post on her Instagram channel (@tanaramsay) thanks to her over 290k followers (as of November 2023).
