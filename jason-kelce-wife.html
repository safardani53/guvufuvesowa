Jason Kelce's Wife Kylie Reveals the Eagles Player Fell Asleep at the Bar on Their First Date

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  662 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Who is Jason Kelce's wife Kylie? The former hockey player opened up about her marriage to the Eagles center on the 'New Heights' podcast.

NFL star Jason Kelce is the center for the Philadelphia Eagles — and there is no denying that his wife is his No. 1 fan.

Jason got his start with the Eagles back in 2011 and has started in every game since aside from four games he missed back in 2014 due to a sports hernia.

Besides being a consistent player, his ability to effectively pass block is a key influence on the success of Jalen Hurts.

The six-time pro-bowler faced off in Super Bowl LVII against his younger brother Travis who plays for the Kansas City Chiefs as a tight end.

It was certainly a family affair at the big game on Feb. 12, as Jason's wife, Kylie McDevitt, brought her OB-GYN as her plus-one because she was less than two weeks away from giving birth to the couple's third daughter. Now, fans will get to learn even more about Kylie and Jason's dynamic in the upcoming Amazon Prime Video documentary, Kelce.

So, who is Kylie Kelce? Keep reading to find out more about the mom-of-three, including how she met her now-husband.

Jason Kelce and his now-wife Kylie McDevitt met on Tinder.

During Jason and Travis's latest episode (aired Sept. 6, 2023) of their New Heights podcast, the NFL star revealed that he and his wife actually met on Tinder. Kylie tells listeners that she swiped right on his profile and met the center at a bar ... where he fell asleep 45 minutes into their date.

Apparently, Jason had a bit too much to drink and eventually had to be carried out by former teammate Beau Allen. Kylie decided to give Jason a second chance and confirmed that date no. 2 went a lot better.

Since meeting on Tinder, Jason tells podcast listeners that he thought Kylie was the "most beautiful woman" and felt like it was "love at first sight."

Jason Kelce's wife Kylie works with the Eagles' Autism Foundation.

During the Sept. 6 episode of New Heights, Kylie explains how she began working as an event consultant for the Eagles Autism Foundation. Kylie opened up about her personal connection to helping those with autism, explaining that her childhood neighbor, who is "more like a brother" is on the spectrum.

According to Kylie's LinkedIn, she has been working with the Eagles Autism Foundation since January 2022.

Jason and Kylie Kelce have three kids.

Jason and Kylie have two daughters, Wyatt Elizabeth, 3, and 2-year-old Elliotte Ray. Kylie shares many pictures of Jason interacting with and caring for their young children, at one point posting an image of their youngest baby asleep on him with the caption "saying fatherhood suits you is an understatement."

The couple welcomed their third daughter, Bennet "Benny" in February 2023.

According to his dad Ed Kelce, all Jason wanted to be growing up was a father, reportedly telling a nurse at 2 years old that he was going to be a father when he grew up.

Amazon Prime Video documentary 'Kelce' will highlight Kelce family's home life.

While we've seen documentaries on what it takes to be a great athlete, we rarely get a glimpse into the personal lives of professional players. In the upcoming Kelce documentary, Kylie and Jason let cameras into their home to document the highs and lows of being married to an NFL player.

In the trailer, Kylie admits that she would like her husband to retire at a time when he can still get "on the floor and play" with his kids. Seemingly one to not shy away from telling her husband exactly how she feels, fans will get to learn a lot more about the woman who stole Jason's heart in the new doc.

Watch Kelce on Sept. 12, 2023 on Amazon Prime Video.
