OASIS by Rachel Stevens has launched, and it's everything our wardrobes needs this Autumn.

The 30 piece collection is a must see for any fashion fan, with autumnal hues, cosy vibes and the right amount of glamour.

Available to shop now from oasisfashion.com, the collection is priced from £32, and is not to be missed.

The 30 piece collection has been designed by Rachel in collaboration with Oasis, creating two core capsules that build the perfect collection on covetable, wear forever pieces.

It's available exclusive from oasisfashion.com and is not to be missed.

The collection has been inspired by Rachel's love of French styling and "pure party decadence", so each piece has been crafted so you feel amazing, and look amazing.
