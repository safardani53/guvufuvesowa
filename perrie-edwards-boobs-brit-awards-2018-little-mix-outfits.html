PERRIE Edwards flashed her boobs and bottom on the Brits red carpet tonight as she joined her Little Mix bandmates.

Perrie, 24, looked sensational in a white sheer dress which featured a plunging neckline and a see-through skirt which revealed her white knickers underneath.

The singer happily posed on the red carpet, blowing kisses and fanning her skirt, clearly unfazed that she was the only member of the girl band who didn't have a date on the red carpet.

While her bandmates Jesy Nelson, Jade Thirlwall and Leigh-Anne Pinnock all arrived with their respective boyfriends, Perrie's partner - footballer Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain - was absent from the red carpet but later joined her inside the venue.

Perrie wasn't the only member of the band to wear white to the Brits, as Leigh-Anne, 26, wore a dressing-gown inspired dress which she wore off one shoulder.

The gown tied at the waist and featured white spots over a sheer fabric, while the thigh high split allowed Leigh-Anne to show off her toned pins.

The singer was joined by boyfriend Andre Gray while bandmate Jesy posed with her fella, Harry James.

Jesy, 26, wore a khaki green wide legged jumpsuit which featured a bustier neckline.

The singer had ditched the dreadlocks she previously showed off on her Instagram, wearing her hair long and straight instead.

Rounding out the foursome was Jade, 25, who looked lovely in a black floor length gown.

The star smiled as she later posed arm-in-arm with her boyfriend, Jed Elliott, who looked smart in a black suit and open necked white shirt.

The night out at the annual ceremony came a day after Jesy was accused of appropriating black culture by angry Little Mix fans after wearing her hair in dreadlocks.

The singer uploaded a snap of her deep auburn hair in dreads on Monday night and instantly sent ripples of anger throughout her fans who branded her “ignorant” and begged her to delete it.
