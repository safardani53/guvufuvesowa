LOVE Island's Noah Purvis confirmed he was kicked off the show due to his porn past and insisted he had "forgotten" about the X-rated videos.

Back in September, The Sun confirmed that he had been fired after fans had found out about his past.

In an interview with TMZ, the 24-year-old revealed the exact moment he realized he had been fired over his past career.

He told the camera: "They're like, 'Well you went by another alias with some nude pictures and videos.'

"As soon as they said that I knew exactly why it happened in my brain. And they had told me that, 'You know, since you were in these videos online, we're not going to be able to have you on the show anymore.'"

He continued: "I just was in complete shock because making sense of it, I had forgotten about these things in my life. I have done these videos for this company and there were pictures taken as well. And honestly, I had forgotten about it.

"And I just thought it was something that was never going to come up again. I started talking to them about how I got into that, like I said 'I unconsciously put it away.'

"And I told him, 'I was told these things were never going to surface.' It was wanting money in the moment and thinking that I was able to somehow put myself out there for producers to see me and put me in movies and stuff like that."

He added: "It was very sad that that wasn't going to happen after the time that I did it."

The former reality TV star then talked about how it's his dream to be on TV and that he was going to do whatever it took to get there.

He said: "I love being in front of the camera, so I'll go on a TV show, a movie, a TV series. Anywhere there's a camera, I'll be there."

Just two weeks ago, The Sun exclusively revealed that Noah had been fired from Love Island after fans accused him of starring in porn.

The Missouri native disappeared from recent episodes with no explanation after he was introduced on the September 17 show. 

His bio on the CBS website was also been removed. 

CBS and ITV America told The Sun in a statement: “It has been brought to our attention that cast member Noah Purvis provided false information on his application to LOVE ISLAND, which violates his contestant agreement.  

“He has been removed from the show.”

Noah’s mysterious absence from the show came amid fans speculating about his possible appearance in porn on social media. 

In the adult videos viewed by The Sun, "Ethan" appears to have the same beauty marks on his shoulders and side as Noah does in Instagram photos. 

They also have similar facial features. 

One fan wrote on Twitter: “If the show kicked Noah out because of his porn past then Imma be pissed. Even though I don't know him and don't really care about him. Sex work is still work. #LoveIslandUSA #LoveIsland."
