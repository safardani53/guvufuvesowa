FARRAH Abraham has been seen cuddling up to her new mystery man while spending the holidays together.

The new photo comes after the Teen Mom star admitted to originally meeting her now-boyfriend on OnlyFans.

Farrah, 32, took to her Instagram Story with a shot of the two standing before a Christmas tree as a "Merry Christmas" sign hung on the wall behind them.

The MTV star looked up at her new boo's face, which was hidden with a heart-eyes emoji sticker.

Her boyfriend's muscular physique could still be seen through his outfit, which consisted of black, short-sleeved shirt.

Farrah opted for a sweater with a fur cardigan overtop, which she paired with knee-high boots.

WHERE'D THEY MEET?

Farrah met her boyfriend on the adult content site OnlyFans in 2021, according to TMZ.

While they had communicated on the platform, things got more serious when they matched on a dating app earlier this year.

In an effort to make sure he wasn't using her for her fame, Farrah made him sign a non-disclosure agreement.

The two began their serious relationship around September, and he purchased a second home close to where Farrah resides.
