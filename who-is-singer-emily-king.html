Who is singer Emily King?

Emily King was born on July 10, 1985, in New York City, New York.

The 37-year-old R&B/Soul musician released her debut album, East Side Story, on August 28, 2007.

King has been nominated for three Grammy Awards, including for Best Contemporary R&B Album for East Side Story at the 50th Annual Awards, Best R&B Song for Look At Me Now at the 62nd Annual Awards, and Best R&B Performance for See Me at the 63rd Annual Awards.

On May 5, 2023, King released her album Special Occasion, which contains 11 tracks.
