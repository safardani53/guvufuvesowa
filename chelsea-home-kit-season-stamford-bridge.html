CHELSEA'S home kit for next season has been leaked on hoardings surrounding Stamford Bridge.

Fans blasted the "hideous" and "lazy" design of the strip after it was revealed online by FootyHeadlines.

It has a huge Three Mobile logo plastered across the front and a Hyundai badge printed on the sleeve.

The strip is predominately the traditional Chelsea blue with with a black 'Pride of London' trim.

Fans slammed the design and had hoped the leaked images were not legit.

But hoardings being put up outside the club's home ground show the players wearing their new kit.
