Im plus size and sick of these tacky clothes trends - cold shoulder tops are the worst

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  223 words  |  Aldo Pusey

ALTHOUGH it's great that more and more clothing brands are stocking plus size options, there are some trends that we can all agree are a miss.

One fashion fan shared some of the most tacky styles on TikTok, and loads of people agree with her picks.

Cassandra Voelker jabbed that there's more than enough 'pizazz' in plus size clothes.

She showed some trendy looks that were completely ruined by the over the top designs, like basic t-shirts with cold shoulder cut outs.

Even comfy hoodies have been ruined by 'pizazz' with added lace sleeves and other 'tacky' designs.

And viewers totally agreed with Cassandra, one joked: "Why is it always the cold shoulder?!"

"Why does being plus size mean I have to be cold," another commented.

And a third wrote: "I saw a plus size blazer on Target's website with open shoulders. A BLAZER!! WHY?!"

Cassandra even found a pair of ripped jeans with lace over the rips, which looked so outdated.

"The jeans legitimately disappointed me because the waistband looks really nice," a viewer said.
