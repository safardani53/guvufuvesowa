Everything to Know About David Woolley Hollywood Life

 Posted on March 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  814 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Christine Brown split from Kody Brown in November 2021.
  • Christine and David Woolley met on a dating app in 2022.
  • The couple revealed that they were engaged in April 2023.

Christine Brown has found her one true love. Nearly two years after leaving Kody Brown following 27 years of spiritual marriage, Christine has married again. Just 6 months after getting engaged, Christine married her fiancé, David Woolley! 

The TLC star hasn’t been shy about showing off her love for her now-husband since the pair went public with their relationship. It’s clear she’s absolutely smitten with David. Get all the details about Christine and David’s wedding, how they met, his family, and more.

When Did Christine and David Get Married?

Christine and David got married on October 7 in Moab, Utah, PEOPLE first reported. They said their vows in front of a whopping 330 guests. “It’s a fairytale,” Christine told PEOPLE about her wedding. “It’s everything I never thought I would have. I get to walk down the aisle to David, him just looking at me the way that he does, it’s a dream come true.”

Christine wore a beautiful white wedding gown from Boda Bridal. The dress featured a plunging neckline and beading details. Christine’s dad and her son, Paedon, walked her down the aisle. After they said “I do,” the newlyweds had Etta James’ hit “At Last” played for them and their guests. Their wedding weekend began with a dinner cruise down the Colorado River.

How Did Christine Meet David?

Like so many relationships in the 2020s, Christine met her now-husband on the internet! Before going official with her beau, the Sister Wives star did reveal she was dating online in an Instagram post in January 2023. “I’m dating again!! Holy. Hell. Awkward. Dating online is crazy!” she wrote.

Long after she and David got engaged, Christine revealed that the couple met on Stir.com, which is a dating website for single parents. “Ironically, Sister Wives just had a commercial break and Stir.com advertised! It’s where I met David,” she wrote on Instagram.

Christine gushed about her fiancé in an Instagram post after a September 2023 episode of Sister Wives. “I’m excited to be marrying @david__woolley, he loves spending time with me every day, doesn’t play games with my heart and wants me to be me. #grateful #soulmate #noplayinggames #loveofmylife,” she wrote.

What Does David Woolley Do for Work?

David opened up a bit more about his personal life in a July 2023 Instagram post. While he shared that many of his children wanted to remain private, he did reveal that he’d been in the construction business for over 40 years. “I have been doing drywall since 1980 and own my company for the last 20 years,” he wrote.

When Did Christine Brown and David Woolley Go Public?

Shortly after opening up about dating online, Christine had teased that she did have a new boyfriend in February 2023. “He’s wonderful and romantic and so kind and everything I’ve been looking for. He’s incredible with [my daughter] Truely. He’s absolutely a dream come true,” she wrote on her Instagram Story after admitting to “dating someone exclusively.” 

Just days after revealing that she had a boyfriend, Christine showed off her new man in a Valentine’s Day post on social media. “I finally found the love of my life, David,” she wrote. “The first time he held me close, it felt like my soul took its first breath. He’s wonderful and kind, incredible with my children and an adorable grandpa. I never dreamed I could find a love like this.”

When Did Christine Brown and David Woolley Get Engaged?

Just two months after going public with their romance, the pair revealed that they’d gotten engaged. “David treats me like a queen and tells me I’m beautiful every day,” Christine told People after their engagement. “I’m so excited for the wonderful adventure we are going to embark on for the rest of our lives.”

Does David Woolley Have Kids?

Christine has gushed about how great David is with her children, and he’s already gone with Christine on a family trip with her daughter, Truely. It’s no surprise that David is also a father himself. He has a huge family just like Christine! His first wife passed away in 2012.

“I have 8 kids. 6 are married 2 are single! I have 10 grandchildren. I also have kids and grandkids in California that are my extended family but I consider my own!” he wrote on Instagram. “Half of my kids do not want to be in the spotlight, so I’m respecting their wishes and you won’t see their faces on here. I was married to their mother for 20 years when depression got a hold of her. So I have been a single dad for 11 years,” he wrote. “I wish I could show all of my family because I’m very proud of all my kids.”
