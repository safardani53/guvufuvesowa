When does Big Nate premiere?
Eight new episodes premiere on Thursday, Feb. 17, and a series of animated shorts are available to stream now on Paramount+.

Who stars in Big Nate?
Big Nate features the voice talents of Ben Giroux (Henry Danger), Dove Cameron (Descendants), Rob Delaney (Catastrophe), Bryce Charles (Blackish), Daniel MK Cohen (LEGO City Adventures), Arnie Pantoja (Final Fantasy VII Remake), Charlie Schlatter (The Loud House), Kevin Michael Richardson (The Simpsons), and Carolyn Hennesy (Lego Star Wars: All-Stars).

What is Big Nate about?
Big Nate tells the story of precocious middle schooler Nate Wright who's on a mission to make middle school more awesome. He's primed for misadventure, but equipped with a loyal group of pals who are always around to help him out of a jam—when they're not getting him into a new one!

The animated series from Nickelodeon Animation Studio is based on the best-selling children’s books and comic strip written and drawn by acclaimed author and cartoonist Lincoln Peirce, executive produced by Mitch Watson (All Hail King Julien) and John Cohen (The Angry Birds Movie) and produced by Amy McKenna (Beware the Batman), with Lincoln Peirce serving as consultant throughout development and production. Bridget McMeel is co-producer.
