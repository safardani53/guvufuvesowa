Today's Jenna Bush Hager life-changing decision may be delayed after huge career announcement

 Posted on April 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  489 words  |  Martina Birk

BELOVED host Jenna Bush Hager may have encountered a huge problem that could interfere with recent moving plans.

Today Show host Jenna has yet to sell her Manhattan apartment which may delay her reported planned move to Connecticut.

The TV personality listed her four-bedroom Tribeca flat in May for $5.375million, according to Sothebys International Realty.

She was the first to live in the brand new apartment complex in 2015 with her husband Henry and their children Mila, Poppy Louise, and Henry "Hal" Hager.

The luxury 2,545 square foot space features a contemporary Eggermanns chef's kitchen, custom cabinets, and a spa-like bathroom, among other amenities.

The family had planned to make the big move from NYC to Connecticut's Fairfield County despite already owning another home on Long Island's North Shore, Hello Magazine reported.

This relocation comes as Jenna is set to make another big move.

BIG ANNOUNCEMENT

Jenna and Hoda hosted the Jenna's Bookclub series and interviewed author Jamie Ford.

He was there to promote his book, The Many Daughters of Afong Moy - a coming to America story, based on real-life events.

As Hoda made the introductions, she announced that Jenna's production company was turning his book into a series.

Most read in Entertainment

"We're going to make this into a series and it's so awesome," Jenna said.

"Jamie, I've been telling Hoda about this book for a long time. I read it a long time ago. I love how it's really about generations of love passed down from mother to daughter."

Jenna is developing the series with UCP and she will serve as executive producer, along with Ben Spector.

She is also adapting Kirk Wallace Johnson’s The Feather Thief: Beauty, Obsession, and the Natural History Heist of the Century.

Jamie told Deadline: "I am beyond excited to be working with Jenna Bush Hager, Ben Spector, and UCP to develop Many Daughters into a series."

“Jenna comes from a long line of book lovers and is a tireless advocate for books, literature, and story. I can hardly think of anyone better to extend that passion into TV."

He added: "Plus, my Chinese grandfather was an extra in Hollywood in the 30s and 40s, appearing in 300+ movies. I just know he’s smiling right now.”

The release date is not yet known.

READ WITH JENNA!

Earlier this month, Jenna teased fans on Instagram when she said she had a big announcement coming.

Her Instagram story read: "Stay tuned for tomorrow's BIG August #readwithjenna announcement. This is one you won't want to miss!"

It marks the return of the Today show book club program Read with Jenna.

Each month, Jenna and members of the Bookclub pick a new book to read together and then discuss it on TV.
