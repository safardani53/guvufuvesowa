NEW HOME

The 5,609 square-foot home sits on a 1.19 acre lot.

The entryway boasts tile flooring and a butterfly staircase.

The first floor also features a living room with a marble fireplace, hardwood floors and a spacious office. 

The gourmet kitchen has a center island with seating, wood cabinets, stainless steel appliances and more amenities. 

The dining area leads to the sprawling and “professionally landscaped” backyard.

The “private oasis,” described by Realtor, features a heated in-ground pool and spa, KOI pond with duel waterfalls and more.

Each bedroom has its own bathroom attached with “ample closet space.”

DONE WITH CHRIS?

Angelina secretly filed for divorce from Chris, 42, in January a little over a year after their 2019 wedding, which was featured on Jersey Shore: Family Vacation.

"I don’t like to hide secrets and hide s**t, so I decide to let my husband know I have these videos and he said he’s good with not seeing them."  

The star continued: "I knew Chris wouldn’t care. He didn’t want to see them. My husband is very well aware of what’s going on in my life and what’s going on in his life. 

"There are no secrets, you know what I mean. This is our marriage."  

The conversation ended on a hopeful note, as Angelina told Chris: “At least we’re moving forward past this.”
