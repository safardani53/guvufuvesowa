This image of a dog's ear supposedly looks like Donald Trump.

When you read the words "Trump's face found in a dog's ear," you might be skeptical. How many people have claimed to see Jesus in a piece of burnt toast, or Abraham Lincoln in an oil spill? But Trump's face is a first. Oddly enough his moniker is actually fairly easy to mock due to his very interesting silhouette and one-of-a-kind "hairstyle," he often is the butt of most jokes. He doesn't take lightly to it though, and we can imagine President Trump is not happy to be compared to something on an animal. But hey maybe people see something different here?

This is not true of this dog's ear. This dog's ear basically has a replica of Donald Trump inside it, and it's terrifying:
