Katie Price backs Theresa May for THIS kinky reason The Sun

 Posted on March 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  239 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

FORMER glamour girl Katie Price has revealed she has more in common with new prime minister Theresa May than you'd think.

Speaking about the politician on ITV's Loose Women, the reality TV star said: "We’ve got something in common, she loves a pair of thigh length boots like me."

The mum-of-five joined the panel to discuss today's papers and immediately spoke of her affinity with the Conservative Party leader.

"What's caught your eye in the papers today Katie?", asked fellow panellist Andrea McLean.

Katie, 38, revealed: "With me it's Theresa May believe it or not. Not in a political way, because you know I try to stay out of that, but it's got to be her shoes.

"Did you see her leopard print shoes? I love them!"

She added: "It just goes to show that it doesn't matter how powerful she is, she's just like one of us girls.

"In fact, we have got something in common, she likes a pair of thigh length boots as well like me."

Commenting on pictures of Katie and Theresa on the screen behind them journalist Janet Street-Porter joked: "Separated at birth.

"All you need is to go and give Theresa a few of your hair extensions."

Katie went on to suggest that it was she who needed "her brain", saying they could "swap tips."

Andrea described that as a "formidable" combination.
