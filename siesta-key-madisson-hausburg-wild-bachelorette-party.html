Inside Siesta Key's pregnant Madisson Hausburg's wild bachelorette party featuring lingerie, strippe

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  548 words  |  Larita Shotwell

SIESTA Key's pregnant Madisson Hausburg and co-star Juliette Porter shared inside videos of her wild bachelorette party featuring lingerie, strippers, and sex toys.

Madisson, 27, and six months along into her pregnancy, is engaged to Ish Soto, 47.

The untamed videos her co-stars posted to Instagram featured Madisson sitting in a chair wearing a miniature wedding veil and a white, pastel floral mini dress.

She was covered in a pile of dollar bills and male strippers danced on either side of her armchair.

Other videos included Madisson's friends sexually dancing with topless strippers, a game of "bra pong" on the wall, and many of the guests stripping down to their bra, panties, and lingerie.

Various sex toys could be spotted as party favors and were also used by the guests.

One post from Kelsey Owens showed a photo of dollar bills, polaroids, and shot glasses on the wooden floor.

She captioned the post: "Things got weird."

In a video from Chloe Trautman, Madisson giggled in her chair and smiled, showing off her ring to the camera.

The clip was captioned: "She's getting marrieddddd!!!!"

'BEYOND EXCITED'

Back in August, the reality star and former Siesta Key producer made the announcement exclusively with PEOPLE and featured photos of her baby bump and ultrasound.

The couple said in a statement to the publication: "We are beyond excited to finally share the news of our baby, due January 1. We already love this little baby so much and can't wait to meet them."

The pair got engaged in August 2020.

HOMEOWNERS!

Last April, The Sun exclusively revealed that Madisson and Ish purchased a $657,000 home in Los Angeles together.

The lavish penthouse condo boasts two bedrooms and two bathrooms. 

The living room has natural light, as it features high ceilings and grand windows. 

Throughout the home are sleek hardwood floors. 

The modern all-white kitchen boasts stainless steel appliances. 

The master bedroom has sliding glass doors, a balcony and an oversized closet. 

The bathroom features a tub, shower and dual vanity. 

Condo amenities include a gym, lounge areas, a pool and more.

Madisson revealed she purchased the home on Instagram last July. 

She captioned a photo of her leaning on her living room table: “I’m officially a freaking HOMEOWNER!!! Following my heart never felt so good! This natural light and these high ceilings are what my dreams are made of.”

Ish commented: “Our new home is almost as beautiful as you!!!.”

Madisson also revealed her new chandelier in the home, as she wrote: “Please excuse my incomplete chandelier. I don’t have a high enough ladder right now to finish putting on the crystals.”

HE PROPOSED

During the reunion special, the TV stars shared that Ish proposed to Madisson shortly after the move.

Host Jeannie Mai asked during the virtual episode how things were going, and that’s when the reality star opened up about the change in her relationship status for the first time.
