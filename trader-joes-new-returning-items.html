TRADER Joe's has just brought back some fan favorites to its stores.

Actress Talia Heskett is a huge Trader Joe's fan and typically shares her favorite finds from the large retailer with her TikTok family.

She has tons of videos on her page reviewing products, sharing meal ideas, and revealing items that have returned.

She recently posted a TikTok where she shared popular returning holiday items.

Just note that items and prices may vary based on location and store.

Below are her six finds starting from least expensive to highest priced.

Sparkling beverage - $1.69

This Elderberry and Pomegranate Apple Cider Vinegar Sparkling Beverage is just in time for the holidays.

The 12-ounce drinks are perfect on their own, for mixers, and only cost $1.69.

Hot Cocoa Polar Bear - $1.99

This classic white and mild chocolate bear is great with milk and perfect for all ages.

Candy Cane Joe Joe's Dark Chocolate Bar - $1.99

This seasonal favorite is made from 60 percent cacao dark chocolate.

The smooth and rich dark chocolate comes with pieces of the classic Candy Cane Joe Joe's cookies and then sprinkled with peppermint candy pieces.

The company said on its website that it makes for a great stocking stuffer or a midday pick-me-up.

Candy Cane Joe Joe's Ice Cream - $3.49

Each pint of this seasonal item delivers a swirl of peppermint and vanilla ice cream.

Then, Trader Joe's added a ribbon of chocolate fudge and its classic Candy Cane Joe-Joe’s Cookies.

The retailer suggests adding the Candy Cane Joe Joe's Dark Chocolate Bar to the mix for even more chocolate goodness.

Date Royale - $7.99

The Deglet Noor dates are filled with peanut butter and then covered in dark chocolate for the perfect snack.

These are gluten-free and each pack comes with 10 pieces.

The seasonal favorite is only 140 calories per serving with 17 grams of sugar.

Facial Sunscreen - $8.99

This sunscreen is definitely a fan favorite as the translucent, oil-free product is formulated for quick absorption.

This means it leaves behind a soft and silky matte finish instead of a greasy residue.

It's an oxybenzone-free, broad-spectrum SPF 40 great for daily use.

Talia said in the comments, "I LOVE it! Feels like satin on my skin. I use it as a primer for my makeup."
