Who is Yacoub Shaheen:

Yacoub Shaheen is a famous World Music Singer. He was born on February 27, 1994 and his birthplace is Bethlehem, Palestine. Yacoub is also well known as, Arabian pop singer who gained fame winning the fourth season of MBC’s Arab Idol.

Yacoub is originated from Palestine. On the fourth season of Arab Idol, he was judged by Wael Kfoury, Nancy Ajram, and Ahlam.
